**NZHike** is your ultimate companion for exploring New Zealand's great outdoors. Whether you are planning a multi-day tramp or looking for a nearby campsite, NZHike provides comprehensive information sourced directly from the Department of Conservation (DOC).

## Features âœ¨

- **Explore Tracks**: Browse detailed information on hiking tracks including difficulty, duration, and length.
- **Find Accommodation**: Locate huts and campsites across the country.
- **Interactive Maps**: View interactive maps for tracks and facilities to navigate with confidence.
- **Weather & Alerts**: Stay informed with local weather updates and alerts for your destination.
- **Favorites**: Save your favorite spots for quick access.
- **Search**: Easily find specific tracks, huts, or campsites.
